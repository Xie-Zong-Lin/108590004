Beverage Shop Introduction

After becoming popular on the Internet, Mr. Liao decided to give back to the society by joining a beverage store. He stated that the franchise fee was only 360,000 yuan (later it was also said that it was more than 600,000 yuan)[15][16], and the franchisee monthly 3,000 yuan must be paid to the head office as a charitable donation, and 10% of the profit will be donated to charitable units. The beverage store opened in April and became the fastest-growing beverage store in mid-2022, with a maximum of 531 franchise stores and two directly-operated stores. However, there was a controversy about the quality problems caused by the fast joining speed of A-Niang's Boss Liao Teahouse. At the end of August, Boss Liao also initiated a trip around Taiwan for A-Niang's Boss Liao's Tea House, visiting every franchise store, and only for them Satisfied store owners give their signatures. Afterwards, he angrily rebuked in the Facebook live broadcast: "Sugarcane tea in some stores is sour!"[17]. Due to the plummeting number of beverage stores, in order to solve the problem and respond to external doubts, he returned the franchise fee of more than 100 stores with uneven quality, with a total value of 140 million. [18] There are currently more than 120 stores remaining.
